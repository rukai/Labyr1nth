Instructions:

  • You are in a maze of twisty little passages, all alike.
  • Use the arrow keys to move around.
  • When a wall is blocking your path, the page will turn black for the time the arrow key is held down.
  • Watch out for grue's. If you get eaten by one, the page will flash.
  • When you reach the exit, the page will turn black. You win!
  • Press r to restart.
